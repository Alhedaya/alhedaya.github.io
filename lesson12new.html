Compulsory Lengthening

ۤ

Refers to specific instances where certain letters or vowels are pronounced for an extended duration, irrespective of the standard rules of pronunciation.
This elongation is mandatory and occurs in accordance with the rules of Tajweed, the set of rules governing the proper recitation of the Qur'aan.
