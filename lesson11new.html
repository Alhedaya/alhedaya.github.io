Long Vowels 
ـَ + ا        ـِ + ي        ـُ + و
The long vowels in Arabic are formed by combining specific short vowel symbols with three letters of the Arabic alphabet:
  • ا is used for the long vowel "aa" when combined with fatha ـَ .
  • ي is used for the long vowel "ii" when combined with kasra ـِ .
  • و is used for the long vowel "uu" when combined with damma ـُ .
